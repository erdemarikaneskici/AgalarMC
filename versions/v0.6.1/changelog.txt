# v0.6.1 Changelog

## Added:
- **Chat Heads** (Player head icons in chat messages.)
- **Chat Tools** (Various chat tools like timestamps or notifications[off by default] etc.)
- **Chat Shot** (Lets you copy or take a screenshot of chat messages.)
- **Shoulder Surfing Reloaded** (Great third person mod with 360 camera movement and freelook.) 
- **Dungeons and Taverns: End Castle Standalone** (Adds a new and rare structure called End Castle using **only vanilla** blocks and mobs to the outer End Islands, in which there are End-themed trial chambers with great loot. Adds a small touch but spices things up a little bit for the end game phase.) 
- **NoFunDNT** (Removes non-vanilla enchants from *Dungeons and Taverns: End Castle Standalone*) 

## Removed:
- **Chatplus** (There were compatiblity issues with other chat mods. But all necessary features and more were added back with *ChatHeads/ChatTools/ChatShot*.)

## Other:
- Updated existing mods.
