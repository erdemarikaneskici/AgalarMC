# v0.4.5 Changelog

## **Added**
- **Euphoria Patches** (Additional features for _**Complementary Shaders**_)
- **Colorwheel** (Replacing the _**Iris & Oculus Flywheel Compat**_ mod)
- **Create Sable Dynamic Lights** (Adds real time light from Sable sub-levels, so for example a lantern at your car can actually light up the non physics blocks around it)
- **Fancy World Animations** (Changes blocks like doors and trapdoors to have an actual animation)
- **Sodium Extra** (Replacing _**Sodium Extras**_)

## **Removed**
- **Iris & Oculus Flywheel Compat** (Replaced with _**Colorwheel**_)
- **SodiumExtras** (_**Sodium Extra**_, another mod, has the necessary funcions and is compatible with _**Reese's Sodium Options**_)

## **Other**
- Updated existing mods.
- Config fixes and UI improvements.
